Outfitter for All Seasons!

We built a web page for seasonal wear outfitters, Eddie Browser. The content changes based on the season, including a look and feel, logo etc.

We used jQuery and created a page that promotes a unique look and feel for each of the four seasons by changing a page in the 4 following areas:

  • A unique color scheme, for example, stylesheet or background color, etc.
  • A unique logo, for example, a picture of a snowflake for winter, etc.
  • A unique item of clothing, for example, a parka for winter.
  • A unique string of text identifying the season, for example, "Leap into our Fall Wear!"
